Frequently Asked Questions

What is Ayurveda?

Ayurveda is an ancient holistic treatment technique that began in India more than 5,000 years ago. It aims to create balance and harmony in the body, mind, and spirit.

What is Panchakarma therapy?

Panchakarma is an Ayurvedic detoxification therapy that aims to remove toxins while restoring balance through five primary procedures like Vaman and Virechan.
